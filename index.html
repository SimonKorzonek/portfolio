About

Made in Cracow, age 26. My adventure with code started on Electronic Data Processing studies on Jagiellonian University in 2015. After 2 years I dropped them to move for some time to Iceland - where I found out that what I want to do in my life, is coding. I learned Python, worked on my portfolio and here I am - searching for the best opportunity to develop my skills in this matter. I'm interested in problems and challenges of Artificial Intelligence, problem solving and making things better.

Currently, alongside with developing one of my Django projects, I'm building my own AI framework in raw Python and NumPy only. The goal is to gain deeper understanding of what and how third-party libraries like Tensorflow are doing under their cryptic hoods.

Native pole with English as second language, basic Icelandic and German. Musician and former music teacher, playing the guitar, the piano and singing. Very much into electronic music. Lover of offroad bikes and sport-touring motorbikes. Huge fan of classic and modern sci-fi, both written and visual. Interested in photography (all photos used in my projects were taken by me).
